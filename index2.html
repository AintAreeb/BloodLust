BloodBane



These blusters are mainly used to attack the opponent. These can be used to kill, imprison or take blood from opponent.



The cards are:





KILLER

NAME: James Wood

ORIGIN: Kyrenia, Cyprus

BIRTH: 07-09-1983

PROFESSION: Serial Killer

MURDER COUNT: 11,289

His real name is James Wood. He is from Cyprus. He is a very notorious serial killer and a big threat to the whole world. He is one of founding member of the criminal organisation of 'Syrus Tenz'. He is very skilled in terms of killing opponent silently. He holds the 4th position in the most wanted list of 'Coperlenz', the world's most prominent Cop Force. He has a high Hex lvl of 2 falling under the group of 'Criminals'. His ability to stealth kill opponent has earned him worldwide fame.







ROBBER

NAME: Hirata Raideen

ORIGIN: Gojo-shi, Nara, Japan

BIRTH: 03-23-1956

PROFESSION: Robber

ROBBERY COUNT: 3,229

His real name is Hirata Raideen. He is from Japan. He is a very notorious and a dangerous robber. He works for the 'Geto Linkers'. He is a very skilled escaper. He escaped 13 times from prison. He is the leader of the squad 'Gaito'. He mostly robs jewelery shops and banks. He is known for being short tempered. One confronting him dies for sure. He has a Hex lvl of 1 falling under the group of 'Criminals'. His endurance and ability to harm opponent earned him great reliability to his masters.







COP

NAME: William Berendsen

ORIGIN: Mondercange, Luxembourg

BIRTH: 11-19-1996

PROFESSION: Cop

CASES SOLVED: 1,172

His real name is William Berendsen. He is from Luxembourg. He is very young and has great potential in him. He is very sensible and gifted with great wisdom. He works for the 'CoperLenz' Cop Force. He already holds the 2nd position in the most cases solved list amongst 'CoperLenz' members. He is the leader of the squad 'Cop-32z'. He has a Hex lvl of 1 falling under the group of 'Law & Order'. His ability to find criminals earned him great reliability among the higher officials.







DEMENTOR

NAME: Umbraeth

ORIGIN: Cueva De Las Minas, Mexico

BIRTH: 793 B.C

PROFESSION: N/A

SOULS REAPED: 739,044

Its real name is Umbraeth. It was born in the cave 'Cueva De Las Minas' in Mexico. It is a member of one of the most terrifying creatures in the wizarding world. These wraithlike beings feed on human happiness, leaving their victims in a state of despair and hopelessness. When it performs the 'Dreaded Dementor’s Kiss', it consumes a person’s soul, leaving them in a permanent vegetative state, often described as a fate worse than death. It is assumed that God sent it to earth giving an objective of taking all the souls of human beings who do not worship God. He is serving God for more than 3000 years till now. He has a high Hex level of 3 falling under the group of 'Entities'. He is a very reliable service provider of his master.







DICTATOR

ORIGIN: Braunau am Inn, Upper Austria, Austria

BIRTH: 04-20-1945

PROFESSION: President

GENOCIDE COUNT: 17 million

He is the president of the country since 1989. He holds the 1st position in the most impactful persons in the world. He was a very successful military officer of 'Bundesheer'. He has a high Hex lvl of 3 falling under the group of 'Law & Order'. He is considered to be the most powerful person in ther world for his intelligency and leadership.

ORIGIN: Medellín, Colombia

BIRTH: 07-22-1985

PROFESSION: Hood

HEIST COUNT: 798
