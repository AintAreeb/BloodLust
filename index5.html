These blusters are mainly used by opponent team to harm vastly. These cards have different kind of nerf characteristics.



The cards are:



SNITCH

NAME: Alexei Orlov

ORIGIN: Moscow, Russia

BIRTH: 09-18-1984

PROFESSION: Snitch

INFORMANT COUNT: 732

Alexei Orlov is a well-known snitch based in Moscow, Russia, born on September 18, 1984. Over his career, he has provided information on 32 high-profile cases involving organized crime and corrupt officials. His role as an informant has made him a significant figure in the realm of intelligence and criminal justice. Known for his willingness to divulge sensitive information, Orlov's actions have had far-reaching consequences for those he has exposed. His reputation as a reliable but controversial source of intelligence has made him a notable and often distrusted figure in the world of covert operations.





TRAITOR

NAME: Victor Dubrowski

ORIGIN: Kyiv, Ukraine

BIRTH: 04-07-1980

PROFESSION: Traitor

BETRAYAL COUNT: 242

Viktor Dubrowski is a notorious traitor from Kyiv, Ukraine, born on April 7, 1980. Known for his role in betraying key allies and leaking sensitive information, Dubrowski has been involved in 17 significant acts of treachery, impacting both governmental and corporate sectors. His actions have caused considerable damage to trust and security, making him a prominent and feared figure in the world of espionage and betrayal. Dubrowski's deceitful actions have earned him a reputation for cunning and duplicity.





NEMESIS

NAME: Kaelgorath

ORIGIN: The Void Between Worlds

BIRTH: 1200 B.C.

PROFESSION: N\A

SOULS REAPED: 112,567

Its real name is Kaelgorath. Emerging from the enigmatic Void Between Worlds, Kaelgorath is a nemesis god of retribution and vengeance. As a force of divine justice, Kaelgorath embodies the relentless pursuit of balance by meting out punishment to those who defy cosmic order. With an origin rooted in ancient mythologies and a role that spans centuries, Kaelgorath maintains a rigorous balance between creation and destruction. Tasked with enforcing the cosmic laws and ensuring that retribution is served, Kaelgorath has remained a formidable entity since its emergence. Kaelgorath is a fearsome and impartial enforcer, ensuring that divine retribution is executed without fail.





SATAN

NAME: Belzahar

ORIGIN: The Abyss, Unknown Realm

BIRTH: Immemorial

PROFESSION: N\A

SOULS REAPED: Countless

Its real name is Belzahar. Originating from the mysterious Abyss, a realm beyond mortal comprehension, Belzahar embodies one of the most feared entities in existence. As the Sovereign of the Damned, Belzahar commands legions of fallen souls and presides over the torment of those condemned to eternal suffering. With an ancient and enigmatic origin, its power and influence have been felt throughout history, often intertwined with tales of rebellion and darkness. Belzahar’s primary role is to oversee the suffering of the damned and ensure the balance between infernal realms and mortal transgressions. It has operated in this role for millennia. Belzahar is a formidable and unwavering force, ensuring the execution of its dark mandate with ruthless efficiency.





DECEIVER

NAME: Lucien Noir

ORIGIN: Paris, France

BIRTH: 11-25-1976

PROFESSION: Deceiver

DECEIT COUNT: 895

Lucien Noir is a notorious deceiver based in Paris, France, born on November 25, 1976. Known for his exceptional skills in manipulation and subterfuge, Noir has orchestrated 89 major deceitful schemes across various sectors, including finance, politics, and personal relationships. His ability to craft intricate falsehoods and exploit psychological vulnerabilities has earned him a reputation as one of the most cunning figures in the art of deception. Lucien Noir’s actions have had significant impacts on those he has targeted, marking him as a prominent and feared figure in the realm of deceit.
