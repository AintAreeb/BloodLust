BloodLust is a fast-paced card game where two teams will be looking forward to eliminate each other. This is a strategy based game.



Basic Rules:

1.The game consists of 32 blusters, 12 from Blood Banes, 10 from Blood Bornes and 1 each from Condemned Souls and Sacred Souls each having unique abilities.

2.Each of the blusters will have a certain amount of bloods. These bloods can be used to play the bluster's ability.

3.Each team will get 11 blusters, 5 from Blood Banes, 4 from Blood Bornes and 1 each from Condemned Souls and Sacred Souls.

4.Using the abilities of these blusters, the team eliminating all the other blusters of the opponent team wins.

5.To eliminate a bluster, one can imprison or directly kill another bluster. There are also the concepts of blocking kill and imprison, time travelling, resurrecting and other exciting moves.

6.Signs you must know:

i. - Hex Level. The team having less hex level after draft gets to play his bluster first.

ii. - Blocked for next move. The bluster having , can't play the card twice consecutively.

iii. - Blocked for 3 moves. A bluster having this will have a cooldown of 3 moves after playing once.

iv. - is blood. These are the bloods that the blusters use to play its ability.

UNBREAKABLE RULES:


1. 'Entities' can only be killed by 'Mystics'.

2. The difference between Hex lvl can't be more than 6.

3. To revive 'Spy', 'Mage' needs 2 bloods.

4. If 'Mecha' is used with 'Witch', two blusters can be poisoned.

5. If 'Mecha' is used with 'Hood', two bloods can be received. 'Mecha' itself can receive, but can't if it dies on the given move.

6. If 'Watchmaker' is harmed by one blood, it can use its last remaining blood and thus, receive the blood lost on the previous move.

7. Ninja's last blood can be used to gain two bloods.

8.If using the last move of the last bluster, opponent's last bluster is killed then, it will be a tie but, if last move can't kill, he'll lose.

9. 'Mystics' even if not having the ability to harm, can harm 'Entities' by one blood. (Imam, Mage can take 1 blood form entities.)

10. 'Skinwalker' can't be killed before it changes its form.

11. 'Sorcerer' can use opponent's 'Hood' to harm and gain blood for sorcerer himself or anyone else.

12. 'Condemned souls' can harm one other. The one using will not be affected
